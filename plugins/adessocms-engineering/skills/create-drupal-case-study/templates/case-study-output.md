## How to Submit on Drupal.org

1. Go to https://drupal.org/node/add/case-study
2. Log in with your Drupal.org account
3. Fill in each field using the sections above
4. Upload screenshots
5. Select appropriate taxonomy terms (Sector, Services)
6. Add team members by Drupal.org username
7. Preview and submit

**Tips**:
- Screenshots should be at least 1200px wide
- Use the "Full HTML" text format for rich formatting
- Link module names to their Drupal.org project pages
- Ensure all team members have Drupal.org accounts
